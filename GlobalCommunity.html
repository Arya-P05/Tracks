Global Community

Online communities where youth from all over the world can gather and engage in discussions that promote Satsang learnings.

Current State:

We currently lack a dedicated online community.

Challenge:

Develop engagement strategies, assess potential platforms, create sample questions or polls, ultimately establish a vibrant online community that serves as a hub for youth to:

  • Explore Satsang teachings in a safe and open forum.
  • Engage in meaningful discussions and share personal experiences.
  • Connect with like-minded peers and build a supportive online community.

Areas of Interest:

Platform Selection: Which platform(s) cater to the preferences of our youth and provide the features for strong online community discussions?

Cultivating Engagement: Interaction and engagement is an absolute must for continued growth in the online community. What strategies can be used to encourage participation?

Moderation and Safety: Given that this will be an online public space, establishing clear guidelines for respectful communication is important. What kind of rules or regulations can we promote to avoid ill-behavior?

  • How will we be able to manage any ill-behavior that occurs? What steps should we take to moderate these forums, and what is the line between appropriate and inappropriate?
  • How can we train a team of moderators who understand Satsang teachings and are comfortable addressing sensitive topics while maintaining a welcoming environment.

Developing a clear code of conduct: Outlining a community's expectations regarding respectful communication, online etiquette, and appropriate content is crucial.

Project Possibilities:

  • Platform ideas
  • Ideas for engagement and creation of a global community
  • Discord/Reddit channel
  • Sample question and answer forums
  • Procedures and processes for responding to questions and queries.
