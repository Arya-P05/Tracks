Local Community

Initiatives taking place in the local communities at the center level. To create close-knit communities that help to build strong relationships and bonds in-person between mukto.

Current State:

Our existing Kishor Sabha, and initiatives such as this Revive Retreat.

Challenge:

Create dynamic and welcoming spaces for youth to build strong connections and ultimately explore Satsang teachings. These initiatives should be able to:

  • Foster Strong Relationships
  • Offer Engaging Events
  • Bridge the Online-Offline Divide

Areas of Interest:

Youth Engagement: What kind of outreach strategies resonate with our targeted demographic? What is our target demographic?

  • Can we understand the interests and preferred communication styles of youth to then attract them to local community events?
  • Would personalized messaging, gamification, or a rewards system work in attracting new participants? How can we harness social media trends or digital marketing tactics to promote these events in a way that feels authentic and relevant?

Event Ideas: Design events in a way to attract new youth and keep our current youth interested.

  • What kind of interesting and engaging events will resonate with youth, and offer a platform for play as well as growth? Could we integrate technology or interactive elements into our events to make them more dynamic? Think of unique ideas such as hackathons, live debates, etc.

Funnels into Satsang: How will we be able to use these local initiatives to create a bigger bond between the mukto but also help them build stronger connections with Satsang and its teachings?

  • How do we maximize these events to help the conversion between event attendance and sabha involvement?

Project Possibilities:

  • Sample posters
  • New and unique event/competition ideas
  • Marketing material
  • Cost breakdowns
  • Feedback loops
  • Outreach strategies to promote the events
  • Engagement Funnels
