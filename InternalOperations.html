Internal Operations

From understanding human behavior to the effective use of technology for providing the smoothest systems, everything internal is dealt with by this team.

Current State:

Asynchronous work culture, Basic onboarding processes, Slack for communication, Google Workspace for collaboration, Google App Sheet and App Script for internal web apps and automation, New database and reporting tools

Challenge:

As a non-profit, spiritual organization, we face unique challenges that require understanding human behavior and effectively using technology to overcome them. How can we support our volunteer force and improve our volunteers’ efficiency, impact, and overall well-being?

Areas of Interest:

Culture and Guidelines: Develop a culture and work style that can support our unique requirements as a non-profit that focuses on personal progress as much as organizational progress.

  • Provide flexibility for our volunteers to work on their schedule while still enabling them to have an impact. How can we utilize technology to support this cultural development?

Data Management and Visualization: Develop systems to manage and make our data accessible.

  • How can we make it easy and secure for our volunteers to find and access the data they need to be successful?

Automation and Workflows: Implement streamlined processes that automate common and repetitive tasks so that our volunteers can focus on what matters.

  • How can we maximize the precious time that our volunteers are dedicating?

Rewards & Recognition: Identify and implement ways to reward and recognize our volunteers for their dedication.

  • How can we appropriately express our appreciation for our volunteers while remaining firm in our principles and values?

Project Possibilities:

  • Using technology to promote a culture of openness
  • Developing an intranet via Google Sites for documentation
  • Proposal for Volunteer or Inventory Management system
  • Innovative attendance or donation collection systems
  • Virtual events and gatherings to improve volunteer well-being and connection
  • “Thank You” packages for volunteers
  • Feedback and mentorship systems to promote volunteer growth
