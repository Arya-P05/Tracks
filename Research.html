Research

As the organization expands globally, the Research Team ensures that we adapt to diverse cultural backgrounds while remaining true to Maharaj’s principles.

Current State:

A specialized team tailors Satsang's practices to resonate with global youth, and maintain cultural representation at all levels. They research cultural differences (India vs. North America), provide cultural education to leadership, and propose Satsang adaptations to address challenges faced by the youth.

Challenge:

As SMVS expands globally, it is connecting with diverse groups of people from various cultural backgrounds. Each group's unique way of life, expectations from Satsang, and spiritual journey are shaped by their cultural context and lifestyle. As an organization, how can we embrace and appreciate this cultural diversity while staying true to Maharaj’s principles and teachings? How can we make Satsang’s practices amicable for a global community?

Areas of Interest:

Cultural Competency: How do we enhance cultural competence amongst senior leadership (including P Santo), center leaders and the youth.

  • What is the process we take to make sure senior leadership within our sanstha has the knowledge and skills to navigate cultural differences with sensitivity and create an inclusive environment?

Research and Adaptation: Conduct research to understand the specific needs and questions arising from cultural variations within Satsang.

  • How do we utilize findings to adapt Satsang’s teachings and program elements to resonate effectively with a multicultural audience.

Open Dialogue: What can we do to include diverse voices and perspectives within Satsang?

  • How do we foster an open and respectful dialogue where youth feel comfortable asking questions about cultural differences, and exploring Satsang at their own pace in a safe space.

Project Possibilities:

As key members of the Research team, you have secured 30 minutes with the senior leadership of the organization. They are keen to learn more about your culture, practices and values to understand you better. What are some things you feel would be beneficial for the senior leadership of the organization to know, and potentially adapt for?

  • Adapting Satsang (ex. Video Games, Platform for Voicing Concerns)
  • Modern Belief Systems (ex. Scientific and Logical Reasoning)
  • Cultural Practices (ex. Cleanliness, Equality, Inquisitive Nature)
  • Lifestyle Choices (ex. Veganism, Minimalism)
