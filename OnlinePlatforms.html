Online Platforms

Responsible for the attraction and retention of youth. This function shares relevant Satsang knowledge on online platforms.

Current State:

@sahajanandiyouth on Instagram, @KaranPhilosophies on Medium & @KaranPhilosophies. on Youtube.

Challenge:

Develop innovative content (ideas and examples), build content strategies for our various platforms and analyze algorithm patterns we can capitalize on. Can you incorporate western media trends to present Satsang teachings in a compelling way?

Areas of Interest:

Trending Topics: To engage with youth, we want topics and messages that resonate with our audiences. What kind of topics are trending and impactful? Analyze popular social media platforms, podcasts, and YouTube channels. Look for trends in content formats, as well as what topics that are relevant to youth and how we can tie that in with Satsang teachings.

Developing Content: Develop engaging content (articles, videos, social media posts) that effectively conveys Satsang teachings while being engaging for today's youth. What are content ideas that might achieve this task?

  • Explore creative formats like short explainer videos, bite-sized social media posts with inspirational quotes, or listicles that apply Satsang principles to common challenges faced by youth.

Content calendar: What kind of strategic plan can be developed for content creation and publishing that ensures engagement? Does timing, place, length of content impact engagement? How should this be planned for different platforms with different algorithms effectively?

  • How to engage the audience on a regular basis. This includes scheduling a regular content release schedule, identifying the most appropriate platforms for each type of content, and creating a strategy to promote and engage with your audience.
  • Consider using social media polls and Q&A sessions to encourage audience interaction and tailor future content to their interests.
