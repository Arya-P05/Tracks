Online Platforms

Responsible for the attraction and retention of youth. This function shares relevant Satsang knowledge on online platforms.

Current State: @sahajanandiyouth on Instagram, @KaranPhilosophies on Medium & @KaranPhilosophies. on Youtube.

Challenge: Develop innovative content (ideas and examples), build content strategies for our various platforms and analyze algorithm patterns we can capitalize on. Can you incorporate western media trends to present Satsang teachings in a compelling way?

Areas of Interest:

Trending Topics: To engage with youth, we want topics and messages that resonate with our audiences. What kind of topics are trending and impactful? Analyze popular social media platforms, podcasts, and YouTube channels. Look for trends in content formats, as well as what topics that are relevant to youth and how we can tie that in with Satsang teachings.

Developing Content: Develop engaging content (articles, videos, social media posts) that effectively conveys Satsang teachings while being engaging for today's youth. What are content ideas that might achieve this task?

Use a Header for Contact Information: Place your name, professional email address, and phone number at the top. You may also include a LinkedIn profile link.

Tailor Your Resume: Customize your resume for each job application. Highlight the experience and skills that align with the job description.

Quantify Achievements: Where possible, use numbers to quantify your achievements. Metrics can provide context and demonstrate the impact of your work.

Avoid Common Mistakes: Steer clear of using first-person pronouns (I, me, my). Double-check for spelling and grammatical errors. Do not include personal information such as age, marital status, or photos unless specifically required.

Use Section Headings: Clearly label each section (e.g., Work Experience, Education) to guide the reader’s eye through your resume.

Optimize for ATS: Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to screen resumes. Use keywords from the job description and a straightforward layout to ensure your resume is ATS-friendly.

Proofread: Review your resume multiple times and consider having someone else look it over to catch any mistakes you may have missed.

By following these guidelines, you can create a well-organized and professional resume that effectively showcases your qualifications and grabs the attention of hiring managers.
